About AI Based E-Memo


AI Based E-Memo is Useful to all Traffic polices in order to reduce their Paper work to making manual Memos. In our project traffic police Scan the Face of Person Who Breaks the Traffic Laws and Gets that person's details from RTO database in his device. After that, traffic police verify required documents of person. Then after based on missing required documents, Traffic police generates E-Memo and send to the person's registered Email address or mobile number.
